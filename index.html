About AcnErase

Created by Martin Hamer, a former nurse and long-time acne sufferer, AcnErase is based on over 22 years of personal research. It empowers users with a scanner and ingredient database to make better-informed choices for their skin.

How It Works

Simply scan a label. AcnErase highlights known acne-related triggers, including hidden dairy and gluten-based ingredients. The app is free, with no ads or sales pitches ‚Äî just a tool to help you take control of your skin and confidence.
